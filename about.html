About Our Company

We are an innovative, reliable smart home development company in Nigeria. We have seen our stunning homes constitute a valuable addition to Nigerian residential architecture since the delivery of our first set of unique and beautifully crafted homes with luxurious interior designs.

We have earned the moniker "luxurious yet comfortable" by designing our homes with the highest standards of quality and keeping our consumers in mind.

We are proud to say that by offering stunning homes in safe and tranquil estates with wonderful aesthetics and cutting-edge technology, we have lifted the standard for what may be deemed luxury.


Kunlad Properties is the Real Estate Campany. Our homes offer occupants a very comfortable home life experience.

Our projects are modeled after rich, luxurious, and classy homes. We have provided our customers with luxurious homes and apartments, as well as top investment opportunities, all of which include fully serviced estate, 24 hour power, 24 hour security, treated water, fire alarm, home automation, and many other home comforts.

You can join the growing number of customers who are now living their dreams in our homes or are simply adding our homes to their portfolio of investment properties with just a 40% down payment of the agreed-upon price of a home. Please check out our projects to see what we've built and what we're selling right now.

At Kunlad Properties, we exist to make your home a joy to live in and a pleasure to own!
